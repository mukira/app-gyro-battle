GyroBattle

This game can be played by one or two players. The app will generate a position, your mission will be to find it with your smartphone. A position is based on 3 values: alpha, beta and gamma.

Your first try will be frustrating, but that's all the beauty of the game. The first player to reach 5 points wins.

Now grab your smartphone and go to the URL below. Once all players are connected, press the button on your remote to start the game.
